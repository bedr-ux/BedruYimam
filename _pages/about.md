# Hey there! 👋 I'm Bedru.

I am a **Computational Speech Processing** researcher and **Language Technology** expert. My work focuses on applying machine learning to solve complex problems in **Speech Technology** [audio modality 💬] and **Natural Language Processing** [text modality 📝]. I am particularly driven by the mission of bringing advanced language technology to the under-represented languages of the world.

### 🎓 Academic Journey
I hold a **PhD in Computational Linguistics** from **Saarland University**. My dissertation introduced novel methods for understanding how **Deep Neural Networks** represent speech variability, providing an interdisciplinary perspective that bridges **Sociolinguistics** and **Machine Learning**. 

Prior to my PhD, I completed my Master’s in the [**LCT**](https://www.coli.uni-saarland.de/lct-coli/) program as a recipient of the prestigious **Erasmus Mundus Scholarship**, funded by the European Commission.

### 🔬 Current Research
Currently, I am a Researcher in the [Language Science and Technology Department](https://www.uni-saarland.de/en/department/lst.html) at **Saarland University**, working alongside [Dietrich Klakow](https://scholar.google.de/citations?user=_HtGYmoAAAAJ) and [Bernd Möbius](https://www.coli.uni-saarland.de/~moebius/mywww/index.html).

### 🌟 Areas of Focus & Collaboration
I am always excited to chat and learn more about:

* **AI for Social Good 🍏** How can we steer AI technologies to create a positive social impact in fields like education and healthcare? What are the unique challenges and possibilities in these domains?

* **Digital Humanities & Heritage 📚** Our ancestors left a wealth of knowledge. Can intelligent language technologies help preserve this rich cultural heritage and make historical wisdom searchable for all curious minds?

* **Language Processing in Humans and Machines 🧠** Can LLMs teach us about language processing in the human brain? How can linguistic theories inspire new computational frameworks for **Neural Network Interpretability**?

* **Multilinguality and Global Inclusion 🌐** Not all languages are fairly represented in today’s tech. How can we develop technologies that are more inclusive and celebrate the linguistic diversity of humankind?
